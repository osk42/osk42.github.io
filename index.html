Concrete Surveillance
is a thought-provoking sculpture crafted from rough concrete. A central pole stands tall, its imposing presence a metaphor for the pervasiveness of surveillance. Mounted on the pole are several circular forms, their smooth surfaces reflecting the ever-watchful gaze of strategically placed CCTV cameras. These rotating circles suggest a sense of unease and constant monitoring, with nowhere to hide from the watchful eyes.
